Résumé

After graduating at GSU, while having success trading stocks and playing poker, I decided to travel the world. During that 5 year period, I think trying to navigate on my own in completely unfamiliar territory and cultures is where I really learned the most.

Eventually being dissatisfied playing zero sum games, I wanted to build something useful and contribute to the world. I've always had a deep curiosity of how different technologies work, so I started working in the tech industry from the ground up.

I believe the best investment you can make is in your own skills, so I decided to learn software engineering by enrolling in DigitalCrafts. That was the best decision I've ever made because I found my passion and I have the ability to build useful things.
